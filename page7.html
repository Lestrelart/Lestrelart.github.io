HELLO, I AM LESTRELA

ESTRELA MEANS STAR IN MY NATIVE LANGUAGE PORTUGUESE, AND THE NAME WITH THE ADDED "L" CAME TO ME IN A DREAM

IN THE FIRST MONTH USING IT, I GOT MORE COMMISSIONS THAN THE PREVIOUS 5 YEARS USING MY OLD NAME

TAKE A SEAT, THIS IS QUITE THE STORY

Undertandable that, being born and raised in the mystical Brazil, I got acquainted at a young age with different people, different tastes and a plethora of experiences only such country can provide

But I also met adverse obstacles from the environment and from myself that led me to a journey of healing and self discovery

At the "late" age of 20, I picked a pencil not with the purpose of doing math related to airplanes, fuel consumption or how does Earth's curvature affects air travel at my Bachelor's in Aviation Science course, but to find out what could I do creatively

My main goal was Realism. The perfectionism that was born from being a woman in a male-dominated field didn't leave me even in an activity whose main goal is to free the mind. I couldn't be less than perfect in my classroom to be taken seriously and it spilled out to other areas of my life.

After graduation, I experienced what many call "The Dark Night of the Soul", where life as you know ends. I was met with Myalgic Encephalomyelitis or Chronic Fatigue, a disease shamefully pushed aside by western medicine. Depression is its best friend and I experienced it at full force. I also experienced loss of a promissing career, isolation, loss of fiends, realtionships and even family. There was nothing left of me. I stayed years homebound and sometimes even bedbound.

Only one thing stayed: my pencils.

After seeking help with every tool I could and after poisoning myself with them resulting in 2 (failed) overdoses, I started experiencing odd events. Repetitions, paterns, spontaneous trance states, strange dreams all neon like. Things that could not be translated in human language.

11:11, 14:14, 17:17

Google searches to figure what was happening led me to a journey no airplane could ever have done. I learned about plant medicine, meditation, altered states of conciouness and energy work. 

Having life swept under your feet has the silver lining of being able to create a new one from scratch. A blank canvas, a Tabula Rasa. And at this point, my artistic skills were at a level of me being called "human printer". On paper, I could create anything I wanted, might as well create a new life with new goals.

Then plant medicine put me in the worst creative block ever, where I finished nothing for some 2 years. I thought I was good, then a whole new world that lies very deep in the human psyche showed itself with each ceremony I attended, something that made me very grateful for being Brazilian. It was another death and rebirth process.

The feeling you can do much more and the weight of being one who's gonna translate imagery, patters, colors and universes the human mind can't yet understand. This was when Lestrela was born ★

Though my Chronic Fatigue is in remission and I'm back at being my sole caretaker, I'm not healed. There are no fast solutions and miracles. I still have bad days that taught me our cyclical nature, taught me to rest, to respect myself, and showed me how much this world is sick mentally, physically and spiritualy. I still believe this missing piece of me being back at full energy will arrive when doctors and researchers exchange their gaslighting for empathy: it almost cost me my life. If I found the perfect balance between science, logical thinking, math, data, flawless technique, intuition, emotions, humanity, love; and apply all of them in my work at the same time, so can they.

Not even the most skilled healers can heal others and their environment but I hope that with my work, I can be a vessel in which creation expresses itself freely. I hope I can give a glimpse that there are much more than the eye can see. Beauty and art has held Empires standing still for centuries, and it can give us the same foundation of strength. I hope my creations make your conciousness itch and takes you on a journey to your own inner universe with your own shining star.

ARTISTS THAT INSPIRE ME THE MOST IN EVERY SENSE OF THE ARTISTIC EXPRESSION ARE DEPECHE MODE, GRIMES, BJÖRK, U2, LEONARDO DA VINCI, IRIS VAN HERPEN, ALEXANDER MCQUEEN, HILMA AF KLINT, HILDEGARD VON BINGEN, ALEX GREY AND GRAFISMO INDÍGENA SUL-AMERICANO

MY FAVORITE VISUAL ARTISTS WHO PUSH ME TO IMPROVE MY SKILLS ARE ARTEMISIA GENTILESCHI, CARAVAGGIO, TANYA SHATSEVA, AND JONATHAN SOLTER

THEY AREN'T IN ORDER, EACH OF THEM HAVE THEIR OWN PLACE AND TIME
