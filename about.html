HELLO, I AM LESTRELA

At the "late" age of 20, I picked a pencil not with the purpose of doing math related to airplanes, fuel consumption versus air pressure or how does Earth's curvature affects air travel at my Bachelor's in Aviation Science course, but to find out what could I do creatively.

I've been improving ever since and out of frustration of how much time a normal sized piece took me (sometimes more than 20 hours) I decided to make miniatures. Then noticed no one else was offering commissions for such product.

MATERIALS

  • PENCILS - Koh-I-Noor 1500 Series (2B, 4B, 6B, 8B), Koh-I-Noor 1860 Series (4B, 6B), Faber Castell 9000 Series (HB, 2B, 4B, 6B, 8B).
  • ERASER - Any brand, preferentially cylindrical shape.
  • BLENDING STUMP - Any brand, must be kept constantly clean. Q-tips also make for a great blending tool.
  • PAPER - 300gsm grammage from V.S.P. Papéis Especiais (Brazilian brand)
  • SHARPENER - A box cutter.

MY FAVORITE VISUAL ARTISTS WHO PUSH ME TO IMPROVE MY SKILLS ARE ARTEMISIA GENTILESCHI, CARAVAGGIO, TANYA SHATSEVA, AND JONATHAN SOLTER
